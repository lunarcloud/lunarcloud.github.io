This website is a portfolio, an exploration of the work of Sam Sarette. It will likely always be a work-in-progress, because Sam will never be done creating and changing. Don't be intimidated by the amount of content here, this should be a fun corner of the web to explore.

The four buttons at the top-right corner are the different variations of this home screen. The four buttons above the starship model (designed by Sam for a game jam project) will take you further into the portfolio. Each location within the portfolio will take you on a different kind of journey. Hopefully, by the end, you will understand Sam and his capabilities, personality, dreams, and hopes.

If you get lost, you can always reach out to Sam on social media, email, etc. You'll likely already have his contact information if you've gotten to this point.
